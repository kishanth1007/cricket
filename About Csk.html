Chennai Super Kings (CSK)

Key Aspects

League: Indian Premier League (IPL)

Home Ground: M. A. Chidambaram Stadium, Chennai (Chepauk)

Captain: Ruturaj Gaikwad (Previously led by MS Dhoni)

Coach: Stephen Fleming

Ownership: Chennai Super Kings Cricket Limited (CSKCL)

Nickname: The Yellow Army, known for their iconic yellow jerseys

Achievements & Records

IPL Titles: 5 championships (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023)

Most Playoff Appearances: 12 times

Most Finals Appearances: 10 times

CLT20 Titles: 2 (2010, 2014)

Win Percentage: One of the highest among all IPL teams

Brand & Culture

CSK is known for its stability, consistent leadership, and long-term trust in players.

The team enjoys one of the largest and most loyal fan bases in India and across the globe.

In 2022, CSK became India’s first sports unicorn, valued at over $1 billion.

Their logo features a roaring lion, symbolizing strength, courage, and fighting spirit inspired by the Tamil Empire.

History

Chennai Super Kings was founded in 2008 as one of the original IPL franchises.

The team faced a two-year suspension from 2016–2017 due to the 2013 IPL betting case.

CSK made a historic comeback in 2018 by winning the IPL title in their return season.
